Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Poster ("we", "our", "the App") is a content management tool that helps TikTok creators upload and manage video content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Poster, we collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: Your TikTok Open ID and display name, obtained through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 authorization flow.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to interact with TikTok's API on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Upload video content to your TikTok account's inbox as authorized by you.
  • Maintain your authenticated session so you don't need to re-authorize frequently.

3. Data Storage

All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your credentials on any external server. Your video files are sent directly to TikTok's servers through their official API.

4. Third-Party Services

Poster interacts with TikTok's Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are stored on your device until you remove your account from Poster or revoke access through TikTok's settings. We retain no data after account removal.

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Remove your account from Poster, which deletes all locally stored tokens.
  • Revoke Poster's access through your TikTok account settings.

7. Security

We use TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication. Tokens are stored locally and are not exposed to third parties.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
